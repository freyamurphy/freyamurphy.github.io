# About Me

I am a final-year PhD student at the [Surrey Centre for Cyber Security](https://www.surrey.ac.uk/surrey-centre-cyber-security), University of Surrey focusing on formal methods and verification for cybersecurity.

I am interested in bridging the gap between theory and real-world impact and evaluating the security of computer systems within a broader context.

## Prior Education

After growing up in the Shetland Islands, I made the transition to city living at the University of Dundee, Scotland, where I completed my BSc (Hons) Computing Science in 2020.

I then moved to Switzerland to study MSc Computer Science at ETH Zürich. In 2023 I wrote my MSc thesis on symbolic models of group and ring signatures with the [Information Security Group](https://infsec.ethz.ch).
